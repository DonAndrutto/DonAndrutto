Tibetan Buddhist Translations

Andrzej R. Rybszleger

English · Polish · Tibetan · Dzongkha

Andrzej R. Rybszleger has served as a translator of Tibetan Buddhist texts for over 20 years, working across liturgical texts, study aids, and commentaries — translating into English and Polish. His translations are published by Yeshe Khorlo under the direction of H.E. Gangteng Tulku Rinpoche. These apps are offered freely as a service to practitioners.
